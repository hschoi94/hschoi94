Use this page as a scratchpad for posts, checklists, or presentation outlines. Below is a starter structure you can clone for new notes.

### Template
- **Context:** What problem are you exploring?
- **Steps:** Bullet out the workflow or commands.
- **Result:** Include screenshots, charts, or links to artifacts.
- **Next:** Capture follow-up questions or improvements.

### Example
- **Context:** Publish a GitHub Pages site for a repo.
- **Steps:**
  1. Update `_config.yml` with `url` and `baseurl`.
  2. Add pages like `about.md` and `projects.md` to the root.
  3. Commit and push; verify the site at `https://<username>.github.io/<repo>/`.
- **Result:** Navigation links are visible and no 404 errors.
- **Next:** Add a blog post under `_posts/` or embed a short demo video.
